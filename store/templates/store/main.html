About

Los Gatos Hacks is a hackathon where hundreds of students from across California will attend. Los Gatos Hacks is a 24-hour in-person event on the weekend of January 20th. If you're looking for an opportunity to flex your tech skills, collaborate with other innovative minds, and work on exciting projects, then you've come to the right place. Over the course of this hackathon, you'll have the chance to build something awesome from scratch, network with industry professionals, and compete for some amazing prizes. But Los Gatos Hacks isn't just about the competition - it's also about the community. We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute, whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out. That's why we've designed this event to be inclusive and supportive, with plenty of opportunities to learn, grow, and connect. So if you're ready to dive into the world of hackathons and see what you're capable of, then join us for Los Gatos Hacks!

Dinner with leaders in tech, Paid Internship Opportunities and $10,000+ of Prizes

Prizes

On the final day of the hackathon, participants will have the opportunity to be recognized for their outstanding contributions and hard work. The top five winning teams will receive exciting prizes but are TBD. It is worth noting that all participants will also recieve a prize for simply participating.

Code

Industry experts from Google, Microsoft, and Nvidia will be present at our hackathon, offering participants the chance to receive feedback, collaborate, and gain insights into the tech industry. Workshops, mentoring sessions, and networking events will allow for meaningful interactions between students and experts, benefiting both parties.

Activities

Our hackathon will feature a Clash Royale and Super Smash Bros tournament in addition to a tech scavenger hunt to promote collaboration, creativity, and strategic thinking among participants. These activities will provide a fun and engaging break from coding while fostering a positive atmosphere for all attendees.

And much more...

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is a Hackathon?

Los Gatos Hacks is a weekend-long event where hundreds of students from around the world come together to create software or design solutions to real-world problems. In teams of three, students will be given a theme and they will have the opportunity to design, build, and publish their own ideas with the help of mentors.

Who can participate?

Any high school or middle school student can participate.

What if I can't code?

No problem! We are accepting people regardless of experience and will be having workshops where you can learn to code

When is Los Gatos Hacks?

Los Gatos Hacks will be a weekend-long hackathon from January 20th-21st, 2024!

Where will Los Gatos Hacks take place?

Los Gatos Hacks will take place in the Los Gatos High School Library (20 High School Ct, Los Gatos, CA 95030 ).

Is it free?

Yes! Thanks to the generous support of our sponsors, Los Gatos Hacks is free for all participants—all food, swag, and other costs are covered.

Registration

Where do I register?

Click here to register!

When is the application deadline?

Registration will open in early June and close on August 31st. Click here to register!

What if I don't have a team?

Don't worry! We'll have team formation events at the beginning of the weekend geared towards helping you find people to work with.

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes! All hackers must follow the MLH Code of Conduct. The packet must be signed in order to attend.

How do teams work?

Your team can have up to 3 people! You can select teammates when you register, and you can also form them during the actual event!

Do you offer travel reimbursements?

Yes! Thanks to the generous support of our sponsors, Los Gatos Hacks is offering travel reimbursements on a case-by-case basis. Please fill out the travel reimbursement form in addition to registering for Los Gatos Hacks.

In Person Logistics

Is Los Gatos Hacks Fully In Person?

Yes. Los Gatos Hacks will be fully in person and in person attendance on both days will be mandatory.

What will I eat?

We’ll provide food for all meals from Saturday’s breakfast to Sunday’s lunch. Of course, we’ll also have an abundance of snacks and drinks throughout the hacking period.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

No problem! If you have dietary restrictions, simply add them to your registration form when you sign up, and we will accommodate for you! Additionally, each meal will have vegan, gluten free, and vegetarian options for everyone.

Miscellaneous

Where do I get more information?

Check out our Devpost and our info page for more info!

I have another question!

Great, we're always open to questions! Feel free to contact us at support@losgatoshacks.com! Or, check out more info about Los Gatos Hacks on our info page!

What should I bring?

Bring a valid student ID, a laptop, chargers, and the signed Parent Packet. There will be a limited amount of laptops that will be available to borrow. Power strips, sleeping bags, eye masks, and toiletries are highly recommended.
