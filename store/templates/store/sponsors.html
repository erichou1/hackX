Mission Statement

We inspire programmers of all skill levels to fall in love with technology and give them the opportunity to learn and apply new skills through the projects they make at our event. We have made it our missino to provide a platform for communities especially those underrepresented in tech to connect, learn, and showcase their skills, and open the world of STEM to those who haven't yet experienced it.

Despite being our first event, we are expecting over 200 attendees this year, with an emphasis placed on including minority groups in STEM.

Since we were founded, we have made it our goal to maintain a 1:1 gender ratio and ensure that students, regardless of their background, can have the opportunity to attend our hackathon and become inspired to pursue a career in STEM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I sponsor Los Gatos Hacks?

Sponsors will have the opportunity to send mentors, present their products, and evangelize their brands at Los Gatos Hacks. Not only will you be able to tap into the limitless potential of the next generation of programmers, but you’ll also be able to make an impactful first impression on our attendees, many of whom are first-time coders. With this opportunity to reach out and advertise to young coders, representatives from your company will also be able to increase your leverage and consumer base. Many of our past sponsors have also found qualified summer interns at our event and were able to successfully recruit these talented minds through our sponsorship program. Our attendees’ performance in the hackathon is itself an excellent indicator of programming skill and creativity.

How can I help?

There are many ways you can support our organization, including donating money directly to Los Gatos Hacks, which allows us the flexibility to ensure that all needs are fully catered to; sending evangelists or engineers to mentor and inspire students; and providing students with the programming tools to use during the event (e.g. hardware, hosting, and API credits).

Where will my money go?

Your gracious donation will be going towards swag for attendees (with your logo on it at the Silver level and above!), prizes, events, and everything else required to run one of nation’s largest student-run high school hackathons. Hack Club, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, is the official fiscal sponsor of Los Gatos Hacks and processes all donations. All contributions will therefore be tax-deductible.
